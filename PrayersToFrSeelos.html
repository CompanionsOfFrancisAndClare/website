Intercessory Prayer to Fr. Seelos

O my God I truly believe you are present with me.

I adore your limitless perfections.

I thank you for the graces and gifts you gave to Blessed Francis Xavier Seelos.

If it is your holy will, please let him be declared a saint of the church so that others

may know and imitate his holy life.

Through his prayers please grant me this favor.

[mention intention]

O Lord, my strength and my Redeemer,

let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart

be pleasing in Your sight

I offer praise to you for the grace You have bestowed

on your humble missionary, Father Francis Xavier Seelos

may I have the same joyful vigor that Father Seelos possessed during his earthly life

to love You deeply and live faithfuly Your gospel.

Amen.
